Process

A short process built to put the result first.

The goal is to move from brief to a page or flow that creates real response without unnecessary rework.

01

Brief

Goal, offer, audience and the action that should happen after the click.

02

Structure

Page map built to highlight the offer, CTA and conversion points.

03

Copy

Headline, short sections and CTA aimed at leads, bookings or contact.

04

Build

Vanilla HTML, CSS and JS with a focus on speed and clean reading.

05

Refinement

Fine-tuning for hierarchy, rhythm and click points.

06

Delivery

A page ready to open clearer conversations.

Typical timelines

Typical timing. No inflated promises.

  • Direct landing page: from a few days to around 2 weeks depending on scope and material.
  • Project with more sections and demos: usually a few weeks.
  • Automations and integrations: timing varies more with rules and testing.

Real timing depends on complexity, feedback rounds and available material.

Main point

Less improvisation, more clarity on what converts

The process exists to reduce noise, keep momentum and avoid endless rounds of changes without direction.
